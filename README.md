# IGNUS Accommodation & Transportation Management System

## Overview

The IGNUS Accommodation & Transportation Management System is a centralized platform designed to manage participant accommodation, transport coordination, room allocation, and volunteer communication during IGNUS, the annual cultural festival of IIT Jodhpur.

The platform helps the accommodation and transportation team manage large participant inflow efficiently by tracking arrivals, departures, room assignments, transport schedules, and emergency contact information.

This project was inspired by real challenges faced during IGNUS’24, where participant rush, last-minute room changes, transport delays, and communication gaps made coordination difficult.

---

## Problem Statement

During IGNUS, hundreds of participants from different colleges arrive at IIT Jodhpur over a short period of time. Managing their accommodation manually creates issues such as:

* Difficulty tracking room availability
* Delays in allocating rooms to participants
* Lack of visibility into arrival and departure schedules
* Poor coordination between PR, transportation, and accommodation teams
* Challenges in maintaining participant safety records
* Lack of a central directory for heads, assistant heads, and volunteers

The goal of this platform is to digitize and streamline these operations.

---

## Key Features

### Participant Management

* Store participant details including name, college, gender, phone number, and government ID
* Record arrival time, departure time, and transport details
* Maintain emergency contact information for safety purposes

### Hostel & Room Allocation

* Separate accommodation flow for boys' and girls' hostels
* Assign hostel, room number, and bed occupancy status
* Track room availability in real time
* Allow quick reassignment in case of room changes

### Entry & Exit Tracking

* Maintain participant entry and exit logs
* Track check-in and check-out time for each room
* Generate reports for current occupancy and vacant rooms

### Rush Forecasting & Planning

* Integrate expected arrival estimates from PR and transportation teams
* Identify peak rush periods based on arrival schedules
* Help teams prepare rooms, volunteers, and transport in advance

### Volunteer & Team Coordination

* Maintain a contact directory for heads, assistant heads, and volunteers
* Assign volunteers to specific hostels, transport routes, or participant groups
* Enable quick communication during emergencies or rush periods

### Dashboard & Analytics

* Total participants arriving and departing
* Hostel-wise occupancy percentage
* Boys vs girls accommodation split
* Peak arrival hours and transport demand
* Volunteer allocation by area
* Daily transport trips and room utilization

## Example Workflow

1. Participant registers for IGNUS
2. PR team shares estimated arrival details
3. Transport team updates bus/train pickup schedules
4. Participant is assigned a boys' or girls' hostel based on gender
5. Room number and bed details are assigned
6. Entry time, exit time, and government ID are recorded
7. Volunteers receive room allocation and participant details
8. Dashboard updates occupancy and rush metrics in real time

---

## Future Improvements

* Add live transport tracking for buses and pickups
* Include WhatsApp/SMS notifications for participants
* Add QR-based room check-in and check-out
* Introduce predictive analytics for peak arrival periods
* Create separate mobile views for volunteers and participants

---

## Impact

This platform would reduce manual coordination, improve room allocation efficiency, strengthen participant safety, and help teams manage rush periods effectively during IGNUS.
